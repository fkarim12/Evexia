×
Carbohydrates: sugars that the body uses for energy. Foods: bread, beans, milk, potatoes.
A person needs 130 g daily. Around 45–65% of one's daily calorie intake should be from carbs.

Protein: helps the body grow, builds muscle and give us energy. Foods: meats, eggs, avocado, nuts.
Females need 46 g and males need 56 g per day. Around 10–30% of their daily calorie intake should be from protein.

Fat: helps the body grow and process vitamins. Foods: chocolate, butter, cheese, bacon.
Around 20%-30% of calorie intake per day should be from fat.
